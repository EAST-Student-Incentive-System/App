About Us

Our platform is designed to make student engagement more rewarding, interactive, and motivating. Built specifically for the University of the West Indies (UWI) community, the app connects students and staff through events, incentives, and friendly competition that encourage active participation in campus life.

Staff members can easily create and manage events and rewards, providing meaningful opportunities for students to get involved in academic, social, and extracurricular activities. Students can join events, log their attendance, and earn points for participating.

Points earned can be redeemed for rewards, allowing students to turn their involvement into tangible benefits. As students accumulate points, they can also earn badges that recognize their level of engagement and achievements.

A dynamic leaderboard allows students to see how they rank among their peers based on total points earned, adding a fun and motivating competitive element to the experience.

Our goal is to strengthen the UWI community by encouraging students to participate, connect with others, and make the most of their university journey while being recognized and rewarded for their engagement along the way.
